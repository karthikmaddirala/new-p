Voyager

Agentic AI Road-Trip Planner

An interactive, map-based road-trip planner driven by two AI agents — one gathers real-world data through tools to plan the trip, the other edits your route live from chat. The models reason; free real-world APIs supply the facts — so every plan is grounded in real ratings, weather, and routing, not vibes.

What makes it interesting
Two agent loops

Genuinely agentic

A tool-use planning agent that decides on its own when to call tools, and a stateful copilot that turns "add Vail, drop Denver" into live edits on the map.

Grounded

Real data, not guesses

Google Places ratings, OSRM drive-time matrix & routing, OpenStreetMap attractions/hotels, Open-Meteo weather, holiday calendars — the LLM reasons over real numbers.

Selection

Fits your actual days

The agent sizes the trip to the real drive-in haul and a brisk sightseeing pace, and ranks stops by blended value (rating × reviews) — no hardcoded "one stop per day."

Human-in-the-loop

You stay in control

The agents curate and act; you make the final call — refine on the map, tick the attractions worth your time, then generate a day-by-day itinerary with real hotels and drive legs.
